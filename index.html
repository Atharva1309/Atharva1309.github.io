    Accomplished my undergraduate in Electronics engineering at one of prestigious university in India. Published a paper on Complex Hand Gesture Recognition at a national conference.

  • 2018 - 2019

    TechBulls SoftTech

    Data Analyst Intern (Pune, India)

    Pre-processed the data to extract the relevant features from dataset with 1 million rows using Pandas and Numpy packages.   • 2019 - 2021

    The University of Texas at Dallas

    Masters in Business Analytics (3.65/4) (Dallas, US)

    Currently pursuing my Master's in Business Analytics with a specialization in Data Science and Machine Learning. Received the Dean's Excellent Scholarship for outstanding academic performance.

  • 2020

    Incture Technologies

    Data Analyst Intern (Pittsburgh, US)

    Imported 500 batches of undigitized (scanned) invoice PDF files to perform OCR. Identified the co-ordinates of the tables containing necessary information to be extracted in the image (scanned) files. Integrated SQL database with Tableau to generate the dashboard and reports for CEO and stakeholders to report sales, progress, and issues contributing to a business use case. Enhanced performance of existing ETL processes and SQL Queries by performing log analysis and query optimization on SQL Server Management Studio and SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS). Improved the invoice data storage for a client by 50% by converting the PDF data to editable format.
